## Common questions

### The camera preview is empty
Make sure Ditty has camera access in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera**. If permission is denied, you can still dither photos from your library — tap the gallery icon (top-left) to upload.

### How do I switch retro systems?
Swipe left/right on the photo, tap any pill in the carousel beneath it, or long-press the photo to open the searchable system list.

### How do I record a GIF?
Press and hold the shutter button. A red ring fills around it as it records (up to 3 seconds). Release to stop and share.

### How do I save my favorite settings for a system?
Open the FX panel, tweak diffuse / ordered / diversity / kernel / palette to taste, then tap **Save** in the preset row. Switching back to that system later will restore the saved values automatically.

### Where are saved photos?
Ditty writes to your Photos library. With **Save original image** turned on (Settings), the unaltered camera frame is also saved at capture.

### What's "Ditty Pro"?
A one-time purchase that unlocks every retro system (free systems include Game Boy, NES, C-64 Multicolor, ZX Spectrum). Pro also turns off the export watermark.

### How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?
Open **Settings → Restore purchases**.
