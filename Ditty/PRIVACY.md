# Ditty — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-08_

Ditty is built and operated by **Other Days Studio** ("we", "us"). This policy
explains what Ditty does and does not collect when you use the app on your
iPhone or iPad.

## Short version

- Ditty does not require an account.
- Ditty does not have a server. We do not collect, transmit, or store your
  photos, videos, or any usage data on our infrastructure.
- Photos and camera frames are processed entirely on your device.
- The only network requests Ditty makes are to Apple, for in-app purchase
  verification.

## What Ditty accesses

| Permission | Why | Where it goes |
|---|---|---|
| Camera | Live retro dithering preview and capture | On-device only, never transmitted |
| Photo Library (read) | Pick a source image to dither | On-device only |
| Photo Library (write) | Save your dithered output (and optionally the original camera shot) | Your own Photo Library |
| In-App Purchases | Unlock "Ditty Pro" — every retro system | Apple's servers (StoreKit), governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) |

We do not have access to your camera or library outside the app, and the app
does not run anything in the background.

## What Ditty does NOT collect

- No analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting SDKs.
- No advertising IDs.
- No third-party trackers.
- No contacts, location, microphone, motion, or health data.

## Saved files

When you tap Save and pick an export aspect, Ditty writes a single image to
your Photo Library on your device. If "Save original image" is enabled in
Settings, a second un-dithered photo is also written. Both are local to your
Photo Library — Ditty never uploads them.

## In-app purchase

Ditty Pro is a one-time purchase processed by Apple's StoreKit. We never see
your payment details. Apple shares with us only the anonymous transaction
identifier needed to confirm your entitlement on each of your devices.

## Data retention

Ditty has no servers, so there is nothing for us to retain. Anything you
delete from your Photo Library is gone from our perspective the moment iOS
removes it.

## Children's privacy

Ditty is suitable for general audiences (rated 4+). Because we collect no
personal data, the app does not knowingly collect anything from anyone of
any age.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy materially changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at
the top and post the new version inside the App Store listing and at the
URL where this document is hosted.
